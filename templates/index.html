Welcome to Academic Tracker

Academic Tracker is a simple web application that allows you to track your academic courses and calculate your GPA.

With Academic Tracker, you can easily add your courses, including the course name, course grade, and number of credit hours. The app will then calculate your GPA based on the courses you've added.

To get started, simply create an account and start adding your courses!

Please note that this web app uses a REST API developed by Eli Freid. This is still a work in progress, so if you encounter any bugs or issues, please don't hesitate to contact me at Eli.freid@gmail.com.

Additionally, please be aware that the site may be slow at times due to server limitations. The server is on a free plan and scales down when inactive, so it may take some time to load or respond during periods of inactivity.
